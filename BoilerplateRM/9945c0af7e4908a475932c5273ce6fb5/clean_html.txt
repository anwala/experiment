Updated Dec. 2, 2016 10:01 a.m. ET
As TV crews and reporters camped out in the lobby of Trump Tower on Monday, recording VIP arrivals in hopes of detecting new members of the incoming Donald Trump administration, a middle-aged corporate executive drew little notice. Greg Hayes, chief executive of United Technologies Corp., wasn’t there to see Mr. Trump.
